01 — The Role

Mike as Co-Executive.

This isn't a sponsorship or influencer deal. Mike embeds into Pause as a Co-Executive — learning every service, its benefits, and its pricing — so he speaks about Pause with the same conviction as the founders.

When Mike introduces someone to Pause, they're not being handed off to a salesperson. They're guided by a trusted source who genuinely believes in the product and can match each person to exactly the right experience for their goals.

Membership — Part of Compensation

Not an add-on. Active use is what makes introductions credible.

Mike's Membership
Full membership included as compensation. Ample credits across all services.
Guest Visits
Mike brings guests for a complimentary single visit — warm, no-pressure first experience.
Service Credits
Monthly allocation across sauna, cold plunge, IV drips, and peptides.
Why It Works
Firsthand knowledge guides guests to the right choices — and real credibility converts.
02 — How Revenue Grows

Three compounding streams.

Most strategies only count new members. This one captures revenue from three directions at once — and they compound month over month.

1
New Member Acquisition
Mike brings warm, trusted introductions from his personal network. Guests are required to post an IG Story tagging Pause — many create Reels or TikToks, compounding reach beyond the visit.
2
Upsell & Add-On Revenue
IV drips, peptides, upgrades. Mike builds systems so every check-in has a revenue touchpoint — turning existing members into higher-value members without acquiring anyone new.
3
Retention & Referrals
Every month a member stays longer is preserved revenue. Every referral they make is a free warm lead. Mike builds the program that makes this automatic.
03 — Commission Structure

Earned on all referred revenue.
